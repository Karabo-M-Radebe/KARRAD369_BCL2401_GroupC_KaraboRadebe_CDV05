Drum Kit

JavaScript sound project

In this project, I was simply exploring what I the world of audio in JavaScript. I used a simple layout in order to draw focus to the main idea of the project and chose to build a drum kit.

The entire project rests mainly on the use of keys to generate the sound with the help of Event listeners assigned to the specific keys.
